The Gallery

  • View in Browser

A 3D gallery straight in your browser. Made with WebGL/ThreeJS for HackNY Fall 2014. It currently obtains its paintings from the Metropolitan Museum of Art Collection Online and these paintings are freely available as they have no copyright. This is a special thanks to the Metropolitan for giving me the priviledge to see all these wonderful paintings in their museum.

These are experiments with the purpose of trying to mix art with programming. It's an effort to create dynamic works that have both artistic properties and random data driven processes. SketchBeat

  • Video
    • |
  • Download
    • |

This was my first visual project after hearing about creative coding through PBS on youtube. I used openFrameworks and C++ and made this in a few hours. It is an audio visualizer program that moves to the beat of the music it is playing. Made in January, 2014, again with good intentions. I plan on re-implementing this in webGL in order to allow portability and remove the OS specific restriction.
